Calm interfaces signal priority, not urgency. They leave room for breathing.

Starts With Tone

Pick one accent and stick to it. Let text do most of the communication; color is for emphasis, not the main language.

Edges and Borders

Use borders sparingly. A single line with generous padding does more than multiple boxes nested inside each other.

Motion

Animations should hint at relationships. A sidebar sliding over the content tells you where it came from and where it will return.

Copy

Short labels reduce clutter. If a tooltip is required to understand a control, reconsider the control.
